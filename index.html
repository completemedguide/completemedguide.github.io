About

This guide covers all you need to know to get into medical school in the UK. It's what we wish we had when we applied.

What is it?

A comprehensive resource that details the medical admissions process. It walks you through it step by step, tells you what you need to do, and how to stand out.

Who is it for?

All school leavers and graduates who want to study medicine in the UK. There's a version for everyone.

The Ultimate Tool.

As we went through the application process, we felt that there was a lack of comprehensive and up to date resources. After in depth research we felt that we could create something that fills this gap. The ultimate tool...

Getting into medical school is one of the toughest things you'll do but we've done our best to make the process as straight forward and simple as possible.

What's Inside?

We've included detailed information on all aspects of the application process. If it's important, you'll find it here.

UKCAT, BMAT & GAMSAT

There's a detailed guide for each exam. Tips, tricks, and all you need to do to achieve the score you want. We've simplified the greatest challenge.

University Selection

We cover the key requirements of all universities and explain how to make the best decisions.

Personal Statement Preparation

What to include, what to avoid, and how to make a great impression. With fully critiqued examples, you'll be sure to write a great piece.

Work Experience

The different kinds of experience, how to get them, and what to do to get the most out of them.

Interview Preparation

60+ full analysed interview questions with model answers. We also discuss how to excel in group tasks, panel interviews and MMI stations.

NHS & Ethics Overview

An overview of the structure of the NHS, medical ethics and hot topics you need to know.
